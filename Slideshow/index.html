Artists Lea Has Seen in Concert

Yes, I know these are some questionable and miscellaneous choices but my music taste has evolved over time. My favorite of these was seeing Jimmy Buffett in 2017 with my mom. I loved Jimmy's music, and still do, and miss him dearly. My favorite song of his is "Fruitcakes". It has a catchy opening and fun chorus that I love to sing and dance to. Another one of my favorite concerts in this slideshow was One Direction. I got to see them in 2015 before they broke up and I saw them at M&T Bank Stadium in Baltimore. It was super fun even though it was nearly nine years ago. I also miss One Direction as much as Jimmy Buffett. I already have many concerts lined up for 2024 and can't wait to add to this list!
